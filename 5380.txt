Public Partnerships I

Amend Rule 2144 by appending the following paragraph:
      If a registered partnership is not a public contract, it can be
      degregistered with Support.

Amend Rule 2178 by replacing:
      Changes in the text of a public contract do not become effective
      until they are published.
with:
      Changes in the text or membership of a public contract do not
      become effective until they are published.
