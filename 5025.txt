R1365 is amended to read:
{
A player may submit a Concurring or Dissenting opinion for a given CFJ
trial or appeals decision with 1 Support. The opinion must reference the CFJ
in question and clearly indicate that it is an opinion to be appended to
that CFJ. The opinion should be accompanied by reasons or arguments. The
Clerk of the Courts shall append any properly submitted opinion to the CFJ
in question, along with a list of the Player who submitted  that opinion and
the player(s) who supported it.
}

Repeal R1626 Applications
