Title: repeal obsolete adjudication provision
Author: Zefram
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Repeal rule 1503.

[R1503 was used to allow the Civil CFJ mechanism to be applied to rule
violations.  Civil CFJs have been abandoned, and the new criminal CFJ
mechanism addresses rule violations directly.  Thus rule 1503 is now
obsolete.]
