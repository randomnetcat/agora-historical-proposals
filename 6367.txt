Title: Low Priority Offices
Author: C-walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Append to Rule 1006 (Offices) the following text:

{{

A low priority office is an office described as such by the rule defining
it. All other offices are high-priority. Rules to the contrary
notwithstanding, the interest index of a low priority office CANNOT be set
to higher than one.

}}
