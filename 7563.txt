ID: 7563
Title: Clarify inaccuracy ban
Author: omd
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Amend Rule 2143 (Official Reports and Duties) by replacing:

      While performing weekly or monthly duties or publishing weekly
      or monthly reports, persons SHALL NOT publish information that
      is Indeterminate, inaccurate or misleading.

with:

      A person SHALL NOT publish information that is Indeterminate,
      inaccurate or misleading while performing a weekly or monthly
      duty, or within a document purporting to be part of any person
      or office's weekly or monthly report.
