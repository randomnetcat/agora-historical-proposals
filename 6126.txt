ID: 6126
Title: No last minute coups
Author: Goethe
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2126 (Notes) by replacing:
     (1) A player CAN spend three Notes forming a major chord to
        increase another non-Alpha player's caste by 1 level.
with:
     (1) A player CAN, except in the last 24 hours of a month, spend
         three Notes forming a major chord to increase another
         non-Alpha player's caste by 1 level.

and by replacing:
     (3) A player CAN spend three Notes forming a minor chord to
         decrease another non-Savage player's caste by 1 level.
with:
     (3) A player CAN, except in the last 24 hours of a month, spend
         three Notes forming a minor chord to decrease another
         non-Savage player's caste by 1 level.
