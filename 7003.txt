ID: 7003
Title: Decision Decisions
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

[ This allows decisions to define their own outcomes, sets the
eligible voters on a decision to be the active first-class players
expect where the rules say otherwise, and removes some superfluous
sections defining voting limits as 1 although they would be anyway
without them. I've checked and none of this clashes with omd's
Soles/Souls proposals. ]

Amend Rule 955 (Determining the Will of Agora) by inserting the
following after paragraph (b) and replacing the second "(c)" with
"(d)".

      (c) If the rule defining a particular type of decision attempts
          to define a method for determining the outcome of such
          decisions, then this method is used.

Amend Rule 1367 (Degrees) by removing ", rules to the contrary
notwithstanding," and setting its power to 1.

Amend Rule 693 (Agoran Decisions) by appending

      The eligible voters on an Agoran Decision are the first-class
      players who are active when the decision is initiated, except
      where the rules say otherwise.

Amend Rule 2154 (Election Procedure) by removing

      2) The eligible voters are the active first-class players.

      3) Each eligible voter's voting limit is one.

and by replacing "4)" with "2)".

Amend Rule 1950 (Voting on Democratic Decisions) by removing

     The eligible voters on a democratic decision are those entities
     that were active first-class players at the start of its voting
     period.  The voting limit of each eligible voter on a democratic
     decision is one.

Amend Rule 1367 (Degrees) by removing

      - The eligible voters are each active player at the time the
        decision is initiated.
