Title: Fix recursive SHOULD
Author: ais523
Co-authors: Sgeo
Adoption index: 3.0

In Rule 2152 (Mother, May I?), replace
{{{
     7. SHOULD, ENCOURAGED, RECOMMENDED:  Before failing to perform
        the described action, the full implications of failing to
        perform it should be understood and carefully weighed.
}}}
with
{{{
     7. SHOULD, ENCOURAGED, RECOMMENDED:  Failing to either perform
        the described action, or to understand and carefully weigh
        the full implications of failing to perform it, violates the
        rule in question.
}}}
