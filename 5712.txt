ID: 5712
Title: Relaxed security
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Change the power of Rule 2110 (Win by Paradox) to 2.

Change the power of Rule 103 (Role of the Speaker) to 1.

Amend Rule 649 (Patent Titles) by replacing this text:

     Awarding or revoking a Patent Title by Proposal is a
     secured change.

with this text:

     Awarding or revoking a rule-defined Patent Title by Proposal
     is a secured change.
