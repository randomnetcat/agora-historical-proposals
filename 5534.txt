ID: 5534
Title: Fix imposed office resignations
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 1006 (Offices) by replacing this text:

      The holder of an office CAN resign it by announcement, causing it
      to become vacant.  As soon as possible after an office becomes
      (or is created) vacant, the IADoP SHALL make at least one
      nomination for the office

with this text:

      The holder of an elected office CAN resign it by announcement,
      causing it to become vacant.  As soon as possible after an
      elected office becomes (or is created) vacant, the IADoP SHALL
      make at least one nomination for the office
