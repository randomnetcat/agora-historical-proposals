 Amend Rule 2362 (Rubles) by replacing this text:

      At the start of each month (after weekly destruction, if it
      coincides with the start of a week), each player gains a ruble
      for each Postulated office e holds.

 with this text:

      At the start of each month (after weekly destruction, if it
      coincides with the start of a week), each player gains a ruble
      for each Postulated office e holds, and each player whose Posture
      has not been supine or leaning at any time during the past month
      gains a ruble.
