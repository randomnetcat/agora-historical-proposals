ID: 7121
Title: Short-term ambassador powers
Author: Tiger
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Amend Rule 2352, replacing the following part:

    When the office of Ambassador first becomes filled, the holder
    SHALL as soon as possible attempt to cause Agora to become a
    player of BlogNomic.

with the following text:

    The Ambassador CAN, with Agoran Consent, act to become a
    player (or the local equivalent) in another nomic on Agora's behalf,
    provided e is not a player in it personally. For each nomic the
    Ambassador is a player of on behalf of Agora, e CAN with Agoran
    Consent act on behalf of Agora to take an available player action
    in that nomic.
