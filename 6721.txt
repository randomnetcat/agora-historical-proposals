ID: 6721
Title: Titularity
Author: coppro
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

PROPOSAL TEXT:
Amend Rule 106 (Adopting Proposals) by replacing:
       A player CAN create a proposal by publishing ("submitting") a
       body of text with a clear indication that it is intended to
       become a proposal, which places the proposal in the Proposal
       Pool.  The author (syn. proposer) of a proposal is the player
       who submitted it.  A player CAN remove (syn. retract, withdraw)
       a proposal e authored from the Proposal Pool by announcement.
with
       A player CAN create a proposal by publishing ("submitting") a
       body of text and an associated title with a clear indication that
       it is intended to become a proposal, which places the proposal in
       the Proposal Pool.  The author (syn. proposer) of a proposal is
       the player who submitted it.  A player CAN remove (syn. retract,
       withdraw) a proposal e authored from the Proposal Pool by
       announcement.
