Mandate II

Amend R2152 by appending the following text:

   8.  If an entity is MANDATED to perform an action or an
       action is a MANDATE for a entity, then the entity
       SHALL perform the action and CAN do so by announcement.
       If no other time limit is specified, e SHALL do so
       as soon as possible.
