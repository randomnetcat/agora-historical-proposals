ID: 6541
Title: Flowers for Wooble 
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 1607 (The Promotor) by replacing this text:

       Distributability is a proposal switch tracked by the Promotor,
       with values Undistributable (default) and Distributable.

with this text:

       Distributability is a switch possessed by proposals that have
       not been distributed, tracked by the Promotor, with values
       Undistributable (default) and Distributable.

Destroy 24 Rests in the possession of the person who was generally
known as Wooble when this proposal was submitted; if e has less than
24 Rests, then destroy all eir Rests.
