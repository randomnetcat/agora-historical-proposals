ID: 6650
Title: Contract Cleanup
Author: coppro
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Amend, in the order in which they are specified in this proposal, Rules
1728 (Dependent Actions), Rule 2166 (Assets), Rule 1586 (Definition and
Continuity of Entities) by removing each instance of "or contract" and
of "or contracts".

Amend Rule 2166 (Assets) by deleting the last paragraph.

Amend Rule 754 (Definition Definitions) by deleting "followed by
definitions used in contracts or other Agoran legal documents,".

If Rule 2263 (Acting On Behalf) has not been repealed, amend it by
removing the last bullet.

If Rule 2199 (Ribbons) has not been repealed, amend it by removing the
last bullet.

Repeal Rule 2285 (Contracts).
