ID: 6319
Title: Relative senatorhood
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2177 (The Senate) by replacing this text:

      A Senator is any first-class player who has been registered
      continuously for the immediately preceding sixty days.

with this text:

      A Senator is any first-class player whose most recent registration
      was at least sixty days ago.
