ID: 6180
Title: Pragmatize decisions properly
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Amend Rule 2034 (Vote Protection and Cutoff for Challenges) by
replacing this text:

       c) (if the matter indicated was whether to adopt a proposal) the
            proposal was not prevented from taking effect.

with this text:

       c) (if the indicated outcome was to adopt a proposal) such a
            proposal existed, was adopted, and took effect.
