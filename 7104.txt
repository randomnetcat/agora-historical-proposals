ID: 7104
Title: Election Spending
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2154 (Election Procedure) by replacing the first paragraph
and the following points labelled a) and b) with:

     A player CAN initiate an election for a specified elected office
     for which no election is already in progress

      a) by announcement, if the office is vacant or assumed;

      b) With 4 Support, or

      c) as a Spending Action, otherwise.

Set the base cost of initiating an election to 10.
