ID: 4815
Title: Really, 200 Rules is Enough
Author: Sherlock
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


   Change the rule entitled "Hard Ruleset Limits" to read:

      If there are 200 or more Rules, and a Proposal is adopted that
      would increase the total number of Rules, the Proposer commits
      the Class 2 Infraction of Improper Rules Fattening, reportable
      by anyone.
