Title: Restore the reasonable unawareness defense
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Amend Rule 1504 (Criminal Cases) by inserting this text before the
paragraph starting with "GUILTY":

      * UNAWARE, appropriate if the defendant violated the specified
        rule via the specified action, but reasonably believed at the
        time that e was not violating a rule
