In rule 2124 (Agoran Satisfaction), replace "A Supporter of a dependent
action is a first-class player who has publicly posted (and not
withdrawn) support for an announcement of intent to perform the action."
with "A Supporter of a dependent action is a first-class player who has
publicly posted (and not withdrawn) support (syn. "consent") for an
announcement of intent to perform the action."
