 Amend Rule 1023 by replacing the text
 {
  Agoran weeks begin at midnight UTC on Monday.
 }
 with
 {
  An Agoran week begins at the start of the first Agoran day that starts
  simultaneous with or later than a the start of a Monday in the UTC
  timezone.
 }
 and replacing the text
 {
  Agoran months begin at midnight UTC on the first day of each Gregorian
  month.
 }
 with
 {
  An Agoran month begins when the first Agoran day contained within a
  Gregorian month begins in the UTC timezone.
 }.
