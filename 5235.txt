Title: No enshrinement of the courts

Amend Rule 2125 by removing item (e), replacing the preceding "; or"
with ".", and appending "or" to item (c).

[If the courts say it's regulated, then it's because the Rules said
so. I don't like 2125(e) because it seems to create a circularity in
the determination of regulated-ness.]
