ID: 5781
Title: Expedited appeals
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2019 (Prerogatives) by replacing this text:

        If the Justiciar has not done so, then the Clerk of the Courts
        SHALL NOT assign a panel to that case during this period.

with this text:

        If the Justiciar has not done so, then the Clerk of the Courts
        SHALL NOT assign a panel to that case during this period,
        unless either no panels eligible to be so assigned include the
        Justiciar, or all of them do.
