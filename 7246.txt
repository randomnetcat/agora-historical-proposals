ID: 7246
Title: Adoption reassociation
Author: omd
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 106 (Adopting proposals) by replacing:

     If the option selected by Agora on a decision about whether to
     adopt a proposal is ADOPTED, then the proposal in question is
     adopted,

with:

     When a decision about whether to adopt a proposal is resolved,
     if the option selected by Agora is ADOPTED, then the proposal in
     question is adopted,
