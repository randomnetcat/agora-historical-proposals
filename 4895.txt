Title: Once is funny, twice would just be annoying
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Amend Rule 2024 (Linked Statements) by appending, after this text:

       Linked CFJs are multiple Calls for Judgement that are submitted
       in a single message and clearly labelled as Linked CFJs.

this text:

       When submitting Linked CFJs, the players (if any) barred by the
       caller from judging the first CFJ are the only players e may bar
       from judging any of the other CFJs.
