ID: 5979
Title: Clarify quorum alterations
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2177 (The Senate) by replacing this text:

     A proposal that ends its voting period in filibuster has a
     quorum of the number of eligible voters plus 1, rules to the
     contrary notwithstanding.

with this text:

     While a proposal is in filibuster, its quorum is the number of
     eligible voters plus one, rules to the contrary notwithstanding.

Amend Rule 2168 (Extending the voting period) by replacing "will
immediately be" with "is instead".
