ID: 6722
Title: Titles can be cleaned too
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

PROPOSAL TEXT:
Amend Rule 2221 (Cleanliness) to read:

       Any player CAN clean a rule without objection by specifying one
       or more corrections to spelling, grammar, and/or capitalization
       in its text and/or title; the rule is amended by this rule as
       specified by that person.
