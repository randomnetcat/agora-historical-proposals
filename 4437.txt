Title: Rouse the Rabble

Adoption index: 1

Amend Rule 1445 (Defaults for Elections) by appending, after this text:

      * Failure of Referendum: If the Referendum fails for any reason
        (e.g. Quorum is not achieved), then the Election has no
        winner.

this text:

        If the Referendum fails because Quorum is not achieved, then
        each Player who was eligible to Vote for the entire Voting
        Period, but did not Vote or declare Presence, commits the
        Class 1 Infraction of Apathy.
