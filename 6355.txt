ID: 6355
Title: Eliminate Annoying Judge Requirements
Author: Yally
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend the paragraph of Rule 1504 (Criminal Cases) reading:

      When a judicial question on culpability is judged after a number
      of rests have been created in the Accused's possession due to
      the associated notice, the judge CAN and SHALL destroy any such
      rests by announcement as soon as possible.

to read:

      When a judicial question on culpability is judged after a number
      of rests have been created in the Accused's posession due to the
      associated notice, those rests are destroyed.
