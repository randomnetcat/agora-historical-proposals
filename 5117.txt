Title: restore deregistration of non-persons
Author: Zefram
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

If proposal 5111 was adopted, amend rule 869 by appending the paragraph

      A player who is not a person and has never been a first-class
      person can be deregistered by any player by announcement.

[This provision was dropped by P5111 in its rewriting of R869.]
