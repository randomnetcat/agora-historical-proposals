Title: Rule Decisions Re Decisions are for Decision Rules
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

[ This could also be used for deciding elections by some system other
than FPTP. ]

Append to Rule 955 (Determining the Will of Agora)

      This Rule defers to the Rule defining a particular decision in
      determining its outcome.

Amend Rule 1367 (Degrees) by replacing

       - The outcome is, rules to the contrary notwithstanding, the
         highest-ranked degree such that more than half the votes cast
         were for that degree or a higher degree, or FAILING GRADE if
         there was no such degree.

with:

       - The outcome is the highest-ranked degree such that more than
         half the votes cast were for that degree or a higher degree,
         or FAILING GRADE if there was no such degree.

and setting its Power to 2.
