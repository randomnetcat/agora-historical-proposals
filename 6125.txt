Title: No more caste sniping
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2211 (The Grand Poobah) by replacing this text:

     At the beginning of each month, each Alpha's caste is flipped to
     eir default value.

with this text:

     At the beginning of each month, the caste of each Alpha who was
     Alpha for at least 14 days during the previous month is flipped
     to eir default value.
