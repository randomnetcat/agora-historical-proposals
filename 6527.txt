ID: 6527
Title: Audit Timing
Author: BobTHJ
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

In R2259 ("Hand Limits") replace:
{{
As soon as possible after the beginning of each month, each
      dealer of a basic deck SHALL by announcement audit each entity
      who owns at least as many cards from that basic deck as any
      other entity.
}}
with:
{{
As soon as possible after the beginning of each month, each
      dealer of a basic deck SHALL by announcement audit each entity
      who at the beginning of that month owned at least as many cards
from that basic deck as any
      other entity.
}}
