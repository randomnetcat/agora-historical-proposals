Title: Defining Monsterholdors
Author: ais523
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Append the following to the rule entitled "The Monster":
{{{
The holder of a Monster is a Monsterholdor, and may be referred to by
the name of the Monster. A Monster is imposed if it is so described by
the rule defining it; otherwise, it is elected.
}}}
