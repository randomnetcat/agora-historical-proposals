ID: 5129
Title: clarify truthfulness
Author: Zefram
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Amend rule 2149 by replacing the sentence

      Players SHALL NOT deliberately or recklessly make false
      statements in any public message.

with

      A player SHALL NOT make a false statement in any public message
      while knowing that the statement is false or being reckless as
      to its veracity.

[Clarifies what the deliberation or recklessness is meant to apply
to.]
