ID: 5595
Title: No more half-hearted causes
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2192 (The Mad Scientist) by replacing this text:

      The Mad Scientist is an office; its holder is responsible for
      building the Monster.  The Mad Scientist CAN act on behalf of
      the Monster to take any action that the Monster may take, and
      SHALL act on behalf of the Monster to ensure that the Monster
      fulfills all of its duties.

with this text:

      The Mad Scientist is an office; its holder is responsible for
      building the Monster.  The Mad Scientist CAN cause the Monster
      to take any action that the Monster may take, and SHALL thus
      ensure that the Monster fulfills all of its duties.

Change the title of Rule 2172 to "Causing Agora to Act", and amend it
by replacing "MAY perform an action on behalf of Agora" with "CAN
cause Agora to perform an action".

Amend Rule 2189 (Win by Extortion) by replacing "acting on behalf of"
with "causing".

Upon the adoption of this proposal, all members of contracts using
phrases like "X CAN act on behalf of Y" SHOULD amend them to "X CAN
cause Y to act".
