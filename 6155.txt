ID: 6155
Title: Refactor falsity
Author: comex
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

WHEREAS Officers hold offices of their own free will, and are
fittingly rewarded for it,
WHEREAS they are relied upon to provide accurate information and
almost unilaterally trusted,
WHEREAS it is appropriate to require from them compliance with the
rules exceeding that of ordinary players,
AND WHEREAS, even if they will normally be DISCHARGED for honest
mistakes, the judge should have the option of providing at least some
penalty for sloppiness,

Rule 2143 (Official Reports and Duties) is amended by adding the paragraph:

     While performing weekly or monthly duties or publishing weekly
     or monthly reports, officers SHALL NOT publish information that
     is inaccurate or misleading.
