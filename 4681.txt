Title: Tiny addition to Illegality
Author: Quazie
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2


Proposal: 'Tiny addition to Illegality'

Replace the following in R1561

    Any Proposal which offers a bribe to a Player or Players to
    vote either FOR or AGAINST a Proposal (either itself or another
    Proposal) shall be completely without effect, even if it is
    adopted, any Rule to the contrary notwithstanding.

with

    If any Proposal  offers a bribe to a Player or Players to vote
either FOR or AGAINST a Proposal (either itself or another Proposal),
then its text shall be completely without effect, even if it is
adopted, any Rule to the contrary notwithstanding.
