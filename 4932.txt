Title: Clarifying Excess CFJs
Author: Levi
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Replace the entire text of rule 2132 with the following text:
     A CFJ submitted by a person who has previously submitted five or  
more CFJs during
   the same Agoran Week as that CFJ is an Excess CFJ. The Clerk of  
the Courts may
   Refuse an Excess CFJ by announcement. A Refused CFJ ceases to be a  
CFJ.
