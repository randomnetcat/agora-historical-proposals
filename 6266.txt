ID: 6266
Title: In case of patient jerks
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: ais523
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2177 (The Senate) by replacing this text:

      Any Senator CAN end a filibuster on a proposal with 4 supporting
      Senators.

with this text:

      Any Senator CAN end a filibuster on a proposal with 4 supporting
      Senators and without objection from a Minister without Portfolio
      (any of whom CAN object).
