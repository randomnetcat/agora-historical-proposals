Title: Down with Distributability
Author: coppro

Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 1607 by doing the following:
 - Replacing the third paragraph with the follosing:
     In a given Agoran week, the Promotor CAN and SHALL, as part of
     eir weekly duties, distribute any proposal that was in the
     Proposal Pool at the beginning of that Agoran Week. The Promotor
     CAN but SHALL NOT distribute any other proposal in the Proposal
     Pool.
 - Deleting the second, fourth, fifth, sixth, and eighth paragraphs.

[Note: without distributability, a Proposal Pool report is not helpful.]
