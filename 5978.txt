Title: Maximum Voting Limits
Author: Elysion
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Create a rule entitled "Maximum Voting Limits" with power 2 reading as
follows:

     Other rules to the contrary notwithstanding, no entity may have
     greater a voting limit than as allowed by this rule.

     The maximum voting limit for any entity on an ordinary decision
     is 8. The maximum voting limit for any entity on any other decision is
     1.
