Title: I
Author: omd

Adoption index: 2.0

Make all elected offices vacant.

Amend Rule 1006 by replacing:

      An imposed office is an office described as such by the rule
      defining it.  All others are elected.

with:

      An elected office is an office described as such by the rule
      defining it.  All others are imposed.
