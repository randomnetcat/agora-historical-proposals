If the proposal titled "Chambers II" fails this proposal has no effect.

Create a new rule titled "Hall of Champions" with Power=1.5 and the
following text:
{{
There exists a Champion Chamber. The eligible voters on a champion's
decision are the players who bear the patent title Champion. The
voting limit of each eligible voter on a champion's decision is equal
to the number of Champion patent titles e holds.
}}

Append to paragraph 3 in R649:
{{
The list of bearers of any rule-defined patent title in the Herald's
report is self-ratifying.
}}
