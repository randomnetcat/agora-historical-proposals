Proposal:SotU's Ephemera
Create a new rule entitled "SotU's Ephemera" with the following text:

   At the end of each month the SotU may award the following
   ephemeral patent titles:

  * Studious, to be given, twice, to the player who checked out the
    most cards from the Library during the month.
