Title: Quick Conclave Fix
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3


Amend Rule 1950 (Voting Limits) by replacing the text:

      The voting limit of an eligible voter on a referendum or an
      election is always one and cannot be changed.

with:

      The voting limit of an eligible voter on a referendum, election,
      or conclave is always one and cannot be changed.
