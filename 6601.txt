Title: Make My Job a Little Easier
Author: Yally
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend the sentence in Rule 2177 (The Senate) reading:

     A Senator is any first-class player whose most recent
     registration was at least sixty days ago.

to read:

     A Senator is any first-class player whose most recent
     registration was at least two months ago.
