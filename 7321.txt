ID: 7321
Title: Appeal of appeals
Author: Murphy 
Co-authors: scshunt
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 1868 (Judge Assignment Generally) by appending this text to
the last paragraph:

    For appeal cases, if possible, e SHALL assign a panel maximizing the
    number of members who have favored it and minimizing the number who
    have disfavored it.
