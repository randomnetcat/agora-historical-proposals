ID: 4792
Title: Regulations Precedence
Author: Manu
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


In the rule entitled "Contracts", amend the part reading:

      A Contract may contain any number of Regulations.

with:

      A Contract may contain any number of Regulations, which must be
      numbered, starting to 1. If two or more Regulations conflict
      with one another, then the Regulation with the lower number
      takes precedence.
