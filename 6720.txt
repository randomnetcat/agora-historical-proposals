ID: 6720
Title: Coauthor Reward
Author: coppro
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

PROPOSAL TEXT:
If Proposal 6710 was ADOPTED, amend the rule it enacted by replacing
       Whenever a proposal is adopted, its author earns a number of
       farads equal to its interest index.
with
       Whenever a proposal is adopted, its author earns a number of
       farads equal to its interest index, and, if it was interested,
       each of its coauthors earns one farad.
