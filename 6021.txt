ID: 6021
Title: Definition of definitions
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Amend Rule 754 (Definition Definitions) by appending this text to
section (2):

         The following clauses, where X and Y are both nouns or
         noun phrases, SHOULD be interpreted as "X is/are defined
         as Y":

           a) "X is/are Y"
           b) "Y is/are known as X"
