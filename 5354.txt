ID: 5354
Title: MMI parallelism
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 2152 (Mother, May I?) by replacing this text:

      4. CAN: Attempts to perform the described action are successful.

      5. MAY: Performing the described action is permitted.

with this text:

      4. CAN: Attempts to perform the described action are successful.

      5. MAY: Performing the described action does not violate the
         rule in question.
