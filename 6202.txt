Title: Actual Periodic Elections
Author: Yally
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Add the following paragraph to the end of Rule 2217 (Periodic Elections):

      As soon as possible after the start of each month, the IADoP SHALL
      intiate an election for the office for which the most time has
      passed since an election was initiated for that office, nominating
      that office's current holder (if any).
