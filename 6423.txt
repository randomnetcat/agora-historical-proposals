Title: Why Can't We Oust Him Already?
Author: coppro
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Amend Rule 2154 by replacing "When an election is initiated, the current
holder of the office (if any) is initially considered to have been
nominated and have accepted." with "When an election is initiated, the
current holder of the office, if an active player, is initially
considered to have been nominated and have accepted."
