Title: Repeal Marks
Author: pikhq
Co-authors: root
Adoption index: 2

Upon adoption of this proposal, for every color of mark, for every player,
each player with 100 or more of a color of mark spends 100 marks to gain one
VC. This process repeats for each player until no more marks can be converted
into VCs.

Repeal Rule 2176.
