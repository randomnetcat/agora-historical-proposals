ID: 7001
Title: Lateness Elections
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2154 (Election Procedure) by replacing

       a) by announcement, if e is the IADoP, or the office is vacant
          or assumed, or no election has been initiated for the office
          within 90 days before the announcement;

with:

       a) by announcement, if e is the IADoP, or the office is vacant
          or assumed, or if the officer has twice failed to complete
          all of eir duties on time in the past two months, or no
          election has been initiated for the office within 90 days
          before the announcement;
