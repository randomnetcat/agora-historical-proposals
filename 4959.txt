Title: Bears as Threats
Author: OscarMeyr
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Create a rule titled "What colour was the bear?" with the following  
text:

Each player whose Agoran nickname or real name contains the word  
"bear", as a word unto itself and not contained in any other word,  
and in any Human language, shall be designated as a Threat of the  
type Bear.
