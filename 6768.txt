Title: damageability
Author: comex
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Amend Rule 2297 by appending this text to the last paragraph:
By default, the current hit points of an Object In Space is equal to
its maximum hit points.  To deal N damage an Object In Space is to
reduce its current hit points by N.  If an object does not have
positive current hit points, it is destroyed.
