Title: Water and oil
Author: Peekee
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Create a Power 3 rule titled "Water and Oil" to read as follows:

Fluid is a switch with values water, oil and emulsion (default).
Players and rules are the only entities that are fluid. Players are
only regulated by rules that are the same fluid as them or emulsion.

Fluid for players is tracked by the Registrar and by the Rulekeepor for
rules.
