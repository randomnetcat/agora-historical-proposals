Proposal: Inactive rights part 1

Replace the following from Rule 1042

      A player may flip another player's noisiness from noisy to
      quiet, unless e has done so for another player within the past
      24 hours.

with

      An active player may flip another active player's noisiness from
      noisy to quiet, unless e has done so for another player within the
      past 24 hours.
