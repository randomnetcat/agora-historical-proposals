Title: Privatize the Welcoming Committee
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Amend the Welcoming Committee contract to read:

     1. The name of this contract is "Welcoming Committee".

     2. Members of this contract SHALL collectively:
        a) Greet new players.
        b) Engage in contact with new players to assist them.
        c) Engage in contact with new players to ensure a positive
           experience.

Repeal Rule 2209 (Agoran Welcoming Committee).
