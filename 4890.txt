ID: 4890
Title: Fix Separation of Powers
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Amend Rule 1450 (Separation of Powers) to read:

       The positions of Speaker, Clerk of the Courts, and Promotor
       are mutually exclusive.  Whenever a player holds more than
       one of these positions, e is removed from all of them except
       Speaker (if he holds it) or the position e came to hold most
       recently (otherwise).
