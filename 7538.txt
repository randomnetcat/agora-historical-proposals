Title: Fencing off

Enact a rule with a power of 3, titled "Historical Rules":

A proposal CANNOT amend Rule 104 "First Speaker" or Rule 2029 "Town
Fountain" unless that proposal explicitly states, using the rule's
title, that it is amending that rule.
