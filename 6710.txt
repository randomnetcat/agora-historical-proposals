ID: 6710
Title: Rewards
Author: coppro
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

PROPOSAL TEXT:
Enact a new Rule entitled "Rewards" at power 2 with the following text:
       Whenever a player judges a case, e earns an number of farads
       equal to its interest index.

       Whenever a proposal is adopted, its author earns a number of
       farads equal to its interest index.
