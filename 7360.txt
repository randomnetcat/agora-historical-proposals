ID: 7360
Title: Proxy Voting
Author: G.
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Create the following Rule, "Proxy Voting", power-3:

      If a player (the Shareholder) holds a controlling interest in
      another player (the Subject), and both the Shareholder and the
      Subject are eligible voters on a given Agoran Decision, then,
      Rules to the Contrary Notwithstanding, the Shareholder CAN
      submit the Subject's ballots on that Decision by announcement.
