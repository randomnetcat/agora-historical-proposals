Title: Controlling Interest
Author: G.
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Create the following Rule, "Controlling Interest", power-3:

      If a first-class player (hereafter the Shareholder) holds
      the majority of existing trading cards that have a single,
      other first-class player as their Subject, then that
      Shareholder holds a Controlling Interest in that Subject.
