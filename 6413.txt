Title: Office IIs with Agoran Consent
Author: c-walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

In Rule 2227 (Interest Index of Offices), replace:

      A player CAN set an office's interest index without objection,
      or without 2 objections if e holds the office.

with:

      A player CAN set an office's interest index without 2
objections, or with Agoran Consent.
