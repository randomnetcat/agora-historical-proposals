ID: 6430
Title: Asset Cleanup
Author: coppro
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2166 by replacing
      The Lost and Found department may own any asset regardless of
      restrictions placed upon an asset by its backing document.
with
      The Lost and Found department may own any asset, and have assets
      transferred to and from it, regardless of the asset's backing
      document or any Rule, Rules to the contrary notwithstanding.
