ID: 6307
Title: Fix NoVs for defined crimes
Author: BobTHJ
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

In R2230 ("Notices of Violation"), replace:
{{
When a Notice becomes Closed, a number
      of Rests are created in the possession of the Accused equal to
      the power of the violated Rule, rounded up.
}}
with:
{{
When a Notice becomes Closed, a number of Rests are created in the
possession of the Accused equal to the Class of the specified Crime,
or in its absence the Power of the violated rule, rounded up.
}}
