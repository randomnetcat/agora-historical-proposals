Title: reintroduce indices
Author: Zefram
Co-authors: Maud
Adoption index: 2

Maud, being the primary author of this proposal but not its submitter,
is hereby named as a coauthor of this proposal.

Enact a rule, titled "Indices", with Power=2, reading:

      Indices are elements of the extended real numbers, which is a
      total order consisting of the real numbers plus a minimum
      element, called negative infinity, and a maximum element, called
      positive infinity or unanimity.
