Title: Super Promotor Powers
Author: omd
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 2350 (Proposals) by appending:

     If a decision of whether to adopt a proposal was resolved as
     FAILED QUORUM in the last seven days, the Promotor CAN once add
     the proposal back to the Proposal Pool by announcement.

Amend Rule 1607 (Distribution) by replacing:

     The distribution of a proposal initiates the Agoran decision of
     whether to adopt the proposal, as described elsewhere.

with:

     The distribution of a proposal initiates an Agoran decision of
     whether to adopt the proposal, as described elsewhere.
