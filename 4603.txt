Title: Card Theft
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Amend Rule 2071 (Wacky Cards) by appending the text:

      * Caption: Robin Hood
        Quota: 1
        Elements: Restricted [Immaculate Players]
        Exploit: For a fee of 3 kudos, you may cause one card to be
          transferred from one player other than yourself (the Rich)
          to another player other than yourself (the Poor), provided
          the Rich has a larger Hand Size than the Poor.

      * Caption: Card Vacuum
        Quota: 1
        Elements: Restricted [Immaculate Players]
        Exploit: For a fee of 2 kudos, you may cause one card in
          the Hand of another player to be discarded.

      * Caption:  Pilfer the Trash
        Quota: 1
        Elements: None
        Exploit:  You may take one card of your choice from the Deck,
          provided that a card of that Class has been discarded in the
          past week.
