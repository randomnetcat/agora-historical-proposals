ID: 5801
Title: Left in a Huff
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.5

Amend Rule 1922 (Defined Regular Patent Titles) by replacing this text:

     (f)  Left in a Huff, to be awarded by the Clerk of the Courts or
          the Registrar (whichever one gets around to it first) to
          any player who publishes a Cantus Cygneus.

with this text:

     (f)  Left in a Huff, to be awarded by the Clerk of the Courts or
          the Registrar (whichever one gets around to it first) to
          any player who deregistered in a Writ of FAGE.
