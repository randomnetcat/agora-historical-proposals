ID: 5578
Title: Easier cleanup of one-off pledges
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: comex
Adoption index: 1.5

comex is a co-author of this proposal.

Amend Rule 2191 (Pledges) by appending this text:

      If a pledge does not impose any ongoing or unsatisfied obligations
      on its current parties, and will not do so in the future in its
      current form, then any person CAN terminate it by announcing that
      it is obsolete.
