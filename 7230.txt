Title: Tax reform

Amend Rule 2362 (Rubles) by replacing:

     At the start of each week, half of each first-class person's
     rubles (rounded down to the nearest integer) are destroyed, then
     two rubles are created in the possession of each first-class
     player.

with:

     At the start of each week, half of each person's rubles (rounded
     up to the nearest integer) are destroyed, then two rubles are
     created in the possession of each first-class player.
