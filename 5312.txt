Title: Protective Alliance
Author: Wooble
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 4

Whereas Agora faces grave danger from devious external forces, and is
in dire need of protection,

Agora submits to Nomicapolis as its benevolent protector.  Nomicapolis
may, at any time, make any changes
whatsoever to the rules or gamestate of Agora.
