ID: 6529
Title: Assumption During Elections
Author: Pavitra
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2276 (Assumption of Vacant Offices) by replacing the text:

                                      provided that both

        (1) the office is either vacant or Assumed, and

        (2) there is no election for that office currently in
            progress.

with the text:

                                      provided that the office is
      either vacant or Assumed.
