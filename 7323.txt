Title: Deprezzing
Author: Machiavelli
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Repeal Rules 2326 and 2348. Amend Rule 103 by removing the paragraph
that begins with "The Speaker CAN, by announcement, cause the
President to take actions that are not otherwise IMPOSSIBLE". Amend
Rule 2347 by removing the sentence "When the Speed has not been
changed in the past month, the President CAN flip the Speed to Normal
or Fast by announcement."
