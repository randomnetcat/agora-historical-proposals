Title: Golem stripping
Author: Machiavelli
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

In the rule "Ribbons", replace the sentence "Ownership of Ribbons is
restricted to players." with "Ownership of Ribbons is restricted to
first-class players. If a Ribbon is owned by an entity that is not a
first-class player, it is destroyed."
