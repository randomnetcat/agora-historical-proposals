ID: 4635
Title: Cards for Proposals
Author: OscarMeyr
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Create a rule with the title "Cards for Proposals" that reads:

  When an Interested Proposal is adopted, the Proposer shall
  gain one pending draw, regardless of any applicable Hand
  Limit.

  When a Disinterested Proposal is adopted, the Proposer shall
  gain one pending draw, subject to any applicable Hand
  Limit.
