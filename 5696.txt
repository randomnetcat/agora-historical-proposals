ID: 5696
Title: Power-Splitting
Author: Goethe
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2211 by appending the following paragraph:

     During the seven days immediately prior to the start of a
     month, the Speaker CAN publish an Honors List, indicating
     a list of players other than emself for promotion.  If such
     a list is published, the Grand Poobah SHALL, during the
     Promotions step above, make as many legal promotions from
     this list as possible before making any other promotions.
