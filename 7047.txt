[Promotor note: marked disinterested.]

[ Not really a fix as such, but this could make the Scorekeepor's life
miserable otherwise. The H. Assessor already includes essential
parameters in eir resolutions (and I'm sure e'd include
disinterestedness anyway) but it's a good idea to mandate it
nevertheless. ]

Amend Rule 1950 (Decisions with Adoption Indices) by appending the
following to the list of essential parameters:

     d) Whether or not the proposal is disinterested.

Amend Rule 208 (Resolving Agoran decisions) by appending the following
to the list of conditions:

     (d) It specifies the essential parameters of the decision.
