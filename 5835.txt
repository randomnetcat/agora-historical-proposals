Title: pledge fixup
Author: Goethe

Adoption index: 2.0

Amend R2178 by appending the following paragraph after the
paragraph labeled (c):

     (d) the contract, or a notice accompanying its publication,
         contains a clause or indication that the contract is
         a pledge.

All pledges that existed immediately prior to proposal 5817 taking
effect, and whose text and membership, as they existed at that time,
had or have been published, are hereby considered to have become
public at that moment.
