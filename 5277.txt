ID: 5277
Title: Secure judicial qualification
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: Zefram
Adoption index: 2

Zefram is a co-author of this proposal.

Amend Rule 1868 (Judge Assignment Generally) by appending this text,
if "Generalize Security" was adopted:

      Making an entity unqualified or poorly qualified to judge is
      secured, with a power threshold of 1.5.

or this text, otherwise:

      An entity CANNOT be made unqualified or poorly qualified to
      judge except as allowed by an instrument with power greater
      than or equal to 1.5.
