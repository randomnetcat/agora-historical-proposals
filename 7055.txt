ID: 7055
Title: Quickly, now
Author: scshunt
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2130 (Activity) by replacing
     A player CAN flip another player's activity to Inactive without
     objection.
with, if there does not currently exist a rule entitled Speed,
     A player CAN flip another player's activity to Inactive without
     objection, or by announcement if that player has not sent a public
     message in more than 10 days.
, otherwise
     A player CAN flip another player's activity to Inactive without
     objection, or by announcement if that player has not sent a
     public message in more than one week if the Speed is Fast, more
     than 10 days if the Speed is Normal, or more than 20 days if the
     Speed is Slow.
