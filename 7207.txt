ID: 7207
Title: Let newcomers vote on proposals
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 1950 (Decisions with Adoption Indices) by replacing this
text:

     The eligible voters on a decision with an adoption index are
     those entities that were active first-class players at the start
     of its voting period.

with this text:

     The eligible voters on a decision with an adoption index are the
     active first-class players.
