Title: Another new prerogative
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2019 (Prerogatives) by appending this text:

         f) Diplomatic Immunity.  If the defendant in a criminal case
            performed the alleged action while e had Diplomatic
            Immunity, then the sentences of APOLOGY and FINE are
            inappropriate, rules to the contrary notwithstanding.

with "f)" changed to "e)" if "A new prerogative" has not been adopted.
