Title: Enough Already
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Amend Rule 408 (Late Judgement) by appending this text:

      If a Judge commits Failure to Judge for two or more Linked
      CFJs, then the penalty for each count beyond the first is
      reduced to 0.5 Blots.

      If a Judge commits Judging a Bit Late for two or more Linked
      CFJs, then the penalty for each count beyond the first is
      reduced to 0.1 Blots.

Upon the adoption of this proposal, the Herald is ordered to
expunge 10 of Sherlock's Blots.

Upon the adoption of this proposal, the Herald is ordered to
expunge 10 of t's Blots.
