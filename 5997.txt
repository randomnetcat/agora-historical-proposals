ID: 5997
Title: Internment
Author: Sgeo
Co-authors: Goethe, Murphy
Adoption index: 3.0

Create a rule with the following text
{
 During an Emergency session, a non-Senator
 may be placed in Internment by the Agoran Consent
 of Senators.

 Internment ends with the end of the Emergency Session.  No
 Senator is ever in Internment, and no player
 is ever in internment outside of an Emergency Session.
 Being in internment is secured.

 When a Player is in Internment, e is not an eligible voter on
 any Agoran Decision, including those that were in their
 voting period when the player was placed in internment, rules
 to the contrary notwithstanding.
}
