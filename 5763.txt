Title: The Endless Repeals: 2136.3
Author: comex
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Amend Rule 2136 (Contests) by removing:
      Notwithstanding the rest of this rule, it is IMPOSSIBLE to flip
      the contestmaster of a contract to a player who is not party to
      that contract; and if a contract's contestmaster ceases to be
      party to that contract, that contract's contestmaster is flipped
      to 'none'.
