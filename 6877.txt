Create a new Power-3 Rule, entitled "Proscription List":

      There is a Proscription List of rules, initially empty.  Any
      person CAN add a rule to the Proscription List by announcement.
      Any person CAN cause this rule to repeal a specified rule in the
      Proscription List by announcement, unless e is the person who
      added it to the List.
