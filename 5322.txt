ID: 5322
Title: Fix Orange VC loss
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2126 (Voting Credits) by replacing this text:

      (-O) When an interested proposal is rejected by voting with no
           valid votes FOR, and having met quorum, its proposer loses
           one orange VC, unless e lost a VC in this way earlier in
           the same week.

with this text:

      (-O) When an interested proposal is rejected by voting with no
           valid votes FOR (other than possibly from its author), and
           having met quorum, its proposer loses one orange VC, unless
           e lost a VC in this way earlier in the same week.
