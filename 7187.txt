ID: 7187
Title: Anyone Can Mislead The Leader
Author: 441344
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2326 by replacing the text {
   A first-class Player CAN cause the President to take actions Without
   Objection, or with Agoran Consent.
 } with {
   A first-class Player CAN cause the President to take actions that
are not
   otherwise IMPOSSIBLE, Without Objection, or with Agoran Consent. Causing
   the President to perform ILLEGAL actions in this manner is the Class-6
   crime of Misleading the Leader.
 }.
