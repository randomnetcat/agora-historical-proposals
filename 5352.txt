Title: Clarify voting limit reduction
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2126 (Voting Credits) by replacing the text:

      c) A player may spend N+1 VCs of different colors to decrease
         another player's VVLOP by N (to a minimum of zero).

with:

      c) A player may spend N+1 VCs of different colors to decrease
         another player's VVLOP by N, to a minimum of zero, where
         N >= 1.
