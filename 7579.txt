ID: 7579
Title: Infraction fix
Author: omd
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2416 (Infraction Cases and Rewards) by replacing:

      A player CAN initiate an infraction case in the same manner as
      an ordinary criminal case, unless the alleged action or inaction
      is defined as a Crime of at least Class 3.

with:

      A player CAN initiate an infraction case in the same manner as
      an ordinary criminal case, unless the alleged action or inaction
      is defined as a Crime of at least Class 3; the case is initially
suspended.

[Per Roujo.]
