Title: Agreement tracking
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Create a rule titled "The Notary" with this text:

      The Notary is an office; its holder is responsible for
      keeping track of agreements between players.

      The Notary's weekly report includes a list of all current
      agreements between players.  The Notary's monthly report
      includes the current text of all such agreements.
