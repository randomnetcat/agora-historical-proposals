Title: Monster Changes

Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.5

Append the following to rule 2193:
{{{
A Monster Change can be one or more of any of the following:

(a) a person who intends to be bound by a Monster becoming a
party to the Monster;

(b) a person ceasing to be a party to the Monster;

(c) amending a Monster; and

(d) terminating a Monster
}}}
