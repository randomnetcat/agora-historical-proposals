Title: Second Class Players Do Not Earn Ergs
Author: Yally
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Amend the text in rule 2282 reading:

      (b) P ergs are created in the possession of each player, where
          P for a player equals 4 plus the sum of the interest
          indices of offices that e holds.

to read:
     (b) P ergs are created in the possession of each player, where P
         equals the sum of the interest indices of offices that e
         holds, plus 4 if e is a first-class player.
