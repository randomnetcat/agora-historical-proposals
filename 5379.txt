Title: Clarify voting limit reduction Mk II

Amend Rule 2126 (Voting Credits) by replacing the text:

      c) A player may spend N+1 VCs, each of a color distinct from the
         rest, to decrease another player's VVLOP by N (to a minimum
         of zero).

with:

      c) A player may spend N+1 VCs, each of a color distinct from the
         rest, to decrease another player's VVLOP by N, to a minimum
         of zero, where N >= 1.
