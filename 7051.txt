Amend Rule 2331 (Proposal Points) by replacing this text:

     However, if the author clearly identified the proposal as
     disinterested when e submitted it, then no one gains or
     loses any points due to its outcome.

with this text:

     However, if more than half the voters clearly indicated
     during the proposal's voting period that it should be
     disinterested, then no one gains any points due to its
     outcome.
