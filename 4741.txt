Adoption index: 1


Proposal: Ephemera from the Promotor
Create a new rule entitled "Promotor's ephemera" with the following text:

    At the end of each month the Promotor may award the following
    ephemeral patent titles:

   * UberProposor, to the player who submitted the best proposal in
     the eyes of the Promotor, during the month.

   * PromotorsBane (Dynamic, but with its Karma set initially to
     Albatross), to the player who removed the most proposals from the
     pool during the month.
