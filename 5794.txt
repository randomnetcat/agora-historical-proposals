Title: Return of the Repeal-o-Matic
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: Swann
Adoption index: 1.0

Create a rule titled "The Repeal-o-Matic" with this text:

     The Anarchist is an office; its holder is responsible for
     proposing the repeal of rules.

     The Anarchist's weekly duties include the performance of the
     following tasks, in order:

       a) Randomly select exactly five distinct rules.

       b) For one to three of these rules, submit a proposal, with
          adoption index equal to the Power of the rule, and
          interest index 0, to repeal the rule.
