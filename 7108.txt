ID: 7108
Title: Urgent Proposals
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 1607 (The Promotor) by appending:

    A player CAN, as a Spending Action, make a specified proposal
    Urgent, provided that they have not already done so in the same
    month. The Promotor SHALL distribute Urgent proposals within
    four days after they become Urgent. If this time limit is
    broken, then any player CAN distribute the proposal.

Set the base cost of making a proposal Urgent to 15.

Amend Rule 107 (Initiating Agoran Decisions) by appending:

    The above notwithstanding, the voting period for a decision
    regarding an Urgent proposal lasts for five days.
