ID: 4506
Title: Will Do
Author: Sherlock
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Amend Rule 1959 (Wills) to read:

      (a) An entity's Will is a description of how the entity's Property
          should be distributed in the event that the entity ceases to
          have an Executor.  An entity may never have more than one Will.

      (b) An entity creates a Will for emself by publishing it.

      (c) An entity changes or destroys eir Will by announcing e does so.

      (d) The Notary shall keep a record of all Wills.
