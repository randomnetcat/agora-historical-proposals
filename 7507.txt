Title: Platonic Fines
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 1504 (Criminal Cases) by appending to the FINE paragraph:
"When the sentence has been in effect for seven days continuously, any
player CAN, if the fine has not already been paid and no player has
already done so, destroy the specified amount of assets in the
possession of the ninny by announcement."
