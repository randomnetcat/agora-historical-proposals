ID: 7245
Title: Proposal reassociation
Author: omd
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 2350 (Proposals) by replacing:

     Once a proposal is created, none of its attributes can be
     changed.  The author (syn. proposer) of a proposal is the person
     who submitted it.

with:

     Creating a proposal adds it to the Proposal Pool.  Once a
     proposal is created, none of its attributes can be changed.  The
     author (syn. proposer) of a proposal is the person who submitted
     it.
