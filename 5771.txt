ID: 5771
Title: Let the CotC decide
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.5

Amend Rule 1871 by replacing this text:

     When the CotC recuses a non-supine player with cause, e SHALL
     flip that player's posture to supine by announcement in a timely
     fashion.

with this text:

     When the CotC recuses a non-supine player with cause, e CAN
     flip that player's posture to supine by announcement in a timely
     fashion.
