Title: Guess how I thought of this
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2154 (Election Procedure) by replacing

       a) by announcement, if e is the IADoP, or the office is vacant
          or assumed, or no election has been initiated for the office
          within 90 days before the announcement;

with:

       a) by announcement, if e is the IADoP, or the office is vacant
          or assumed, or if a player has deputised for the office
          since its last election, or no election has been initiated
          for the office within 90 days before the announcement;
