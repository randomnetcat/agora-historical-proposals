Title: Points for the Big Guy
Author: Tanner L. Swett
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Create a power-2 rule, titled "Public Appointment", with the following text:

At the beginning of every month, half of the points belonging to the
Lost and Found Department, rounded up, are transferred to Agora. Any
player CAN, with three support, act on behalf of Agora to announce
intent to initiate a victory case.
