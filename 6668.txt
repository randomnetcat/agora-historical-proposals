Title: Legislative and judicial salaries
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2282 (Energy) by replacing section (b) with this text:

        (b) For each first-class player, 4+O+P+J ergs are created in
            eir possession, where

              i) O is the sum of the interest indices of offices
                 that e holds (0 if none),

             ii) P is the maximum interest index among the proposals e
                 authored that were adopted during the past week (0 if
                 none), and

            iii) J is the maximum interest index among the judicial
                 cases that e judged on time during the past week (0 if
                 none).
