ID: 6174
Title: Ratification Accuracy
Author: woggle
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Amend Rule 2202 (Ratification Without Objection) by appending a new
paragraph:
{{
     A player SHALL NOT knowingly use or announce intent to use
Ratification Without Objection to ratify
     a (prior to ratification) incorrect document when a corrected
document could be produced with reasonable
     effort. Such ratification or announcement of intent to ratify is
the Class-8 Crime of Endorsing Forgery.
}}
