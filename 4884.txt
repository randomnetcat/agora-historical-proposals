Title: Credit for Service
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3


[YAFI, YGI.]

Amend Rule 2126 (Voting Credits) by appending, after this text:

       When a proposal is adopted, its proposer gains VCs equal to the
       integer portion of the proposal's adoption index, and each
       co-author of the proposal gains one VC.

this text:

       At the end of each quarter, for each office, the player (if
       any) who held that office for the majority of that quarter
       gains one VC.

       A player who submits a judgement during eir Deliberation Period
       gains one VC.  A player whose judgement is overturned loses one
       VC, if e has any.
