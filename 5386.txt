ID: 5386
Title: Revise criminal defenses
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: Zefram
Adoption index: 1.7

Amend Rule 1504 (Criminal Cases) by replacing the paragraph
containing "EXCUSED, appropriate if" with this text:

      * UNAWARE, appropriate if the defendant reasonably believed
        that the alleged act did not violate the specified rule

      * EXCUSED, appropriate if the defendant could not reasonably
        avoid breaching the rules in a manner at least as serious
        as that alleged
