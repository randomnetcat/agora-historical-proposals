Title: Fix election quorum
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2154 (Replacing Officers) by replacing this text:

      quorum is a minimum of three and the number of active players

with this text:

      quorum is a minimum of three and the number of active players
      (rules to the contrary notwithstanding)

[Otherwise it gets overruled by Rule 879.]
