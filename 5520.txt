ID: 5520
Title: Fix OVERLOOKED
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: Ivan Hope
Adoption index: 1.7

Ivan Hope is a co-author of this proposal.

Amend Rule 1504 (Criminal Cases) by replacing this text:

      * OVERLOOKED, appropriate if the alleged act allegedly occurred
        at least 200 days before the case was initiated

with this text:

      * OVERLOOKED, appropriate if the alleged act did not occur within
        200 days before the case was initiated
