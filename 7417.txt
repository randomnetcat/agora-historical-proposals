ID: 7417
Title: Blackmail is no fun
Author: Roujo
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

In Rule 2370, "The Ambassador-At-Large", replace:
    {
        The Ambassador-At-Large SHALL inform any nomic that receives
such a claim of its falsehood as soon as possible.
    }
    with
    {
        The Ambassador-At-Large SHALL, as soon as possible after he
becomes aware of such a claim, make a reasonable effort to inform any
nomic that received it of its falsehood.
    }
