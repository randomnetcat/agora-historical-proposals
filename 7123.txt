ID: 7123
Title: Restore stereotypes
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.7

Amend Rule 911 (Appeal Cases) by appending this text after the list
beginning "The valid values of <action>":

     An opinion of AFFIRM or REMAND implicitly specifies "without
     prejudice", and an opinion of REMIT or OVERRULE implicitly
     specifies "with prejudice", unless the opinion explicitly
     specifies otherwise.
