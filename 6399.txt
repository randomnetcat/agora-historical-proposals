ID: 6399
Title: decriminalize self-recusals
Author: Wooble
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

In Rule 2158, replace:
      When a judicial question is applicable and open, and its case
      has a judge assigned to it, the judge CAN assign a valid
      judgement to it by announcement, and SHALL do so as soon as
      possible.
with:
      When a judicial question is applicable and open, and its case
      has a judge assigned to it, the judge CAN assign a valid
      judgement to it by announcement, and SHALL do so as soon as
      possible, unless e is recused from the case before the time limit
      for doing so has expired.
