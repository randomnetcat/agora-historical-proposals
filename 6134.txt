ID: 6134
Title: Advanced Notice
Author: Goethe
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

[Adds a useful dependent mechanism]

Amend Rule 1728 (Dependent Actions) by, immediately after:
           3) With N Agoran Consent, where N is an integer multiple
              of 0.1 with a minimum of 1.
inserting:
           4) With Advanced Notice.


Amend Rule 1728 (Dependent Actions) by replacing:
                                         (if the action depends on
         objections) at least four days earlier.
with:
                                         (if the action depends on
         objections or advanced notice) at least four days earlier.


Amend Rule 2124 (Agoran Satisfaction) by inserting, immediately after
the paragraph labeled (3), the following paragraph:

     (4) if the action is to be performed with Advanced Notice,
         then it has any number of supporters or objectors,
         including zero.
