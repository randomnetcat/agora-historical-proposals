ID: 6352
Title: Begin at the beginning
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Amend Rule 2244 (The Games of Agora) by replacing this text:

                                 The "first game" was the period of
      time prior to the first instant at which a person won the game.

with this text:

                                 The "first game" was the period of
      time between June 30, 1993, 00:04:30 GMT +1200, and the first
      instant at which a person won the game.
