Title: Titleless Proposal
Author: RedKnight

Amend Rule 1952 by replacing the text "equal to half the amount of the
Distribution Cost of the Proposal, rounded up." with "equal to one-fourth
the amount of the Distribution Cost of the Proposal, rounded up to the
nearest integer multiple of the MUQ of Papyrus."
