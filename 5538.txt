Amend Rule 106 (Adopting Proposals) by adding this paragraph after the
first one:
      Proposals can include a title, which is a string of characters.
      When submitting a proposal, the proposer CAN and SHALL set its
      title.

Amend Rule 1607 (The Promotor) by replacing:
      a) Its author (and co-authors, if any).
      b) Its interest index.
with:
      a) Its author (and co-authors, if any).
      b) Its title, if any.
      c) Its interest index.
