ID: 5797
Title: Office with care
Author: Taral
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend rule 2154 by replacing the text:

     A consenting candidate is a player who was nominated during the
     first half of the nominating period and did not refuse, or who was
     nominated during the second half and accepted.

with the text:

     A consenting candidate is a first-class player who was nominated
     during the first half of the nominating period and did not refuse,
     or any player who was nominated during the nominating period and
     accepted.
