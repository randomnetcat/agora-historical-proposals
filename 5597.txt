Title: Breaking Contracts
Author: Sgeo
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.5

Create a Power=1.5 rule titled "Breaking Contracts" with the following text
{{
Rules to the contrary notwithstanding, a player bound by a contract or
pledge CAN, by announcement, leave any contract e is bound by at any
time. E MAY do so if and only if the contract or pledge e is leaving
does not explicitly prohibit em leaving the contract, rules to the
contrary notwithstanding.
}}
