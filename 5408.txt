ID: 5408
Title: In a timely fashion
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 1023 (Common Definitions) by replacing this text:

      (a) The phrase "as soon as possible" means "within seven days".

with:

      (a) The phrases "in a timely fashion" and "as soon as possible"
          mean "within seven days".


Amend Rule 2019 (Prerogatives) by replacing this text:

      As soon as possible after the beginning of the month, the
      Speaker SHALL randomly assign each Minister Without Portfolio a
      different Prerogative for the remainder of that month.

with:

      In a timely fashion before the beginning of each month, the
      Speaker SHALL randomly assign each Minister Without Portfolio a
      different Prerogative for the upcoming month.
