Title: Foreign trade
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Create a rule titled "Foreign Trade" with this text:

      A player CAN spend some of eir assets to export them to a
      foreign nomic; e SHALL inform that nomic of the export as
      soon as possible, preferably by simultaneously sending the
      announcement to an appropriate foreign forum.

      The Ambassador SHOULD encourage foreign nomics to adopt
      legislation recognizing Agoran exports by creating comparable
      foreign assets.

      Players are encouraged to adopt legislation recognizing foreign
      exports by creating comparable Agoran assets.

Create a rule titled "Trade Embargo" with this text:

       A player SHALL NOT export assets to a foreign nomic unless its
       Recognition is Protected, Friendly, or Neutral.
