In recognition of achievement of excellence in an illustration of
self-amendment that has spawned a tradition spanning 23 years (and
more yet to come), the players of Agora do hereby confer upon
Peter Suber the Honorary Degree of Doctor of Nomic Philosophy and
do hereby give testimony of fulfillment of all the requirements of
the Degree.


In recognition of outstanding work in the field of thriller fiction,
the players of Agora do hereby confer upon Steven Swiniarski the
Honorary Degree of Master of Nomic and do hereby give testimony of
fulfillment of all the requirements of the Degree.
