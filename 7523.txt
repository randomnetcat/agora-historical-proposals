ID: 7523
Title: remove dangling judicial declaration reference
Author: omd
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 2170 (Who Am I?) by replacing:

      (Upon a judicial finding that the Executor of a public
      message cannot otherwise be determined within reasonable effort,
      the judge SHALL in a timely fashion publish a judicial
      declaration specifying the identity of that message's Executor.)

with:

      (Upon a judicial finding that the Executor of a public
      message cannot otherwise be determined within reasonable effort,
      the judge SHOULD attempt to ratify a document specifying eir
      identity.)
