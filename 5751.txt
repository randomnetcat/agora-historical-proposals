Title: Platonic Poobah

Adoption index: 1.0

Amend Rule 2211 by replacing:
     The Grand Poobah is an office; its holder is responsible for
     keeping track of castes.
with:
     The Grand Poobah is an imposed office; its holder is responsible
     for keeping track of castes.
and by replacing:
          a) Flip a player's caste to Alpha
with:
          a) Flip a player's caste to Alpha; when a player's caste is
             flipped to Alpha, e becomes the Grand Poobah
