Title: Give those back
Author: Machiavelli
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

In the rule "Belligerence", after the paragraph beginning "Minerals
are a currency", add the following paragraph:

      If a person who is not a player owns units or minerals, any
      person CAN transfer any of those units or minerals to the Lost
      and Found Department without objection. The owner of those units
      or minerals is eligible to object to this action. If a person
      who owns units or minerals does not intend to use them, e SHOULD
      transfer them to the Lost and Found Department.
