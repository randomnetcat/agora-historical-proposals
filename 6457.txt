ID: 6457
Title: Increase Proposal Card Gains
Author: C-walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

In Rule 2261 (The Deck of Change) replace

      When a proposal authored by a player is adopted and takes
      effect, that player earns a number of draws from the Change Deck
      equal to the Interest Index of the proposal.

with:

      When a proposal authored by a player is adopted and takes
      effect, that player earns a number of draws from the Change Deck
      equal to the Interest Index of the proposal plus one.
