ID: 6345
Title: Time travel
Author: Pavitra
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3.0

Amend Rule 478 (Fora) by replacing the text:

           Any action performed by sending a message is performed at
      the time date-stamped on that message.

with this text:

           Any action performed by sending a message is performed at
      the time specified in the message, if any, or at the time
      date-stamped on that message otherwise.
