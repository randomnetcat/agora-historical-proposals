ID: 6267
Title: Herald Must Announce a Change in Speaker
Author: Yally
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.0

Amend Rule 402 (Identity of the Speaker) by adding the following to
the end of the first paragraph:

      As soon as possible after the Speaker changes, the Herald SHALL
      announce this fact along with the identity of the new Speaker
      and the date of the change.
