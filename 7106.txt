ID: 7106
Title: Veto
Author: Walker
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Enact a new Rule entitled "Veto" with the following text:

     Players CAN, as a Spending Action, Veto a specified ordinary
     decision in its voting period. When a decision is Vetoed its
     adoption index is increased by one.

Set the base cost of Vetoing an ordinary decision to 25 points.
