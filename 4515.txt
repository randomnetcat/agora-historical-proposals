ID: 4515
Title: Expunge Nomic Properties
Author: Maud
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3


Amend rule 1274 (Definition of Indices) to read:

      The indices are the nonnegative real numbers with their usual
      order, along with the special value Unanimity.  Unanimity is
      greater than any other index.

Amend rule 594 (Power and Proposals) by striking the text that reads: 

      For the purpose of the Rules, the application of an adopted
      Proposal is a legal procedure for changing Nomic Properties.

Repeal rule 1011 (Definition of Nomic Property).
