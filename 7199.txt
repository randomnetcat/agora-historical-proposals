ID: 7199
Title: Allow late appeals without objection
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1.7

Amend Rule 911 (Appeal Cases) by replacing this text:

     An appeal concerning any assignment of judgement in a non-appeal
     case within the past two weeks CAN be initiated by any player
     with 2 support.

with this text:

     An appeal concerning any assignment of judgement in a non-appeal
     case CAN be initiated by any player with 2 support and (if the
     judgement was assigned more than two weeks earlier) without
     objection.
