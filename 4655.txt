Title: Slow Play Reporting
Author: Kolja
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1


Amend 1686 by replacing 
"Each officer with a Weekly Report must publish it weekly; each" 
with
"Each officer with a Weekly Report must publish it weekly unless the
content of the report would substantially be the same as in the
previously published report. In this case, a report must only be
published within one week of the next substantial change to the
report's content. Each"
