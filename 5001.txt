ID: 5001
Title: Putting the bar back into barrister
Author: root
Co-authors: Quazie
Adoption index: 1

Quazie is a co-author of this proposal.

Amend R897 by replacing the section reading:

     A Player Barred from Judging a CFJ is ineligible to Judge that
     CFJ.

with:

     A player barred from judging a CFJ is ineligible to judge that
     CFJ.  The caller of a CFJ is automatically barred from judging
     it.
