ID: 5088
Title: Compensate for vacant offices and speaker as an office
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Amend Rule 1006 (Offices) by replacing this text:

      If an officer fails to satisfy a Timing Order to perform a
      certain official duty, than the player who executed the order
      may perform the duty as if e were the officer.

with this text:

      If a Timing Order directed at an office is not satisfied on time,
      or if the office is vacant, then the player who executed the order
      may perform the duty as if e held that office.
