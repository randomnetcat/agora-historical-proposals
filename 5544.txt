Title: Justice is Injustice
Author: ais523
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.1

Add the following power=2.1 rule:
{{{
CFJ 1984 SHALL NOT and CANNOT be given a judgement.

Any appeals of CFJ 1984 SHALL be judged either REMAND or REASSIGN,
and any attempts to give any other judgements to such appeals are
ILLEGAL and IMPOSSIBLE.

Notwithstanding any other rules, CFJ 1984 CAN be appealed by any
player by announcement, even if it has been appealed previously.

Players SHALL NOT and CANNOT be punished for failure to give a
judgement to CFJ 1984.

Players SHALL NOT and CANNOT be recused from CFJ 1984 with cause.
}}}
