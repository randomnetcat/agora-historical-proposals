Title: Deflate Blue Marks
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Upon the adoption of this proposal:

1) Each player loses Blue Marks equal to eir Blue Mark holdings
   rounded down to the nearest multiple of 100.

2) Each player who spent Blue Marks to gain Blue VCs since the
   submission of this proposal loses Blue VCs equal to the number
   of Blue VCs that e gained in this way.

[Simplicity over exactitude.]
