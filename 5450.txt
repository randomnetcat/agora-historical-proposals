ID: 5450
Title: Optional is optional
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 3

Amend Rule 208 (Resolving Agoran decisions) by replacing this text:

      The vote collector for an unresolved Agoran decision may resolve
      it by announcement, indicating the option selected by Agora.  E
      SHALL do so as soon as possible after the end of the voting
      period.

with this text:

      The vote collector for an unresolved Agoran decision CAN resolve
      it by announcement, indicating the option selected by Agora.  If
      it was required to be initiated, then e SHALL resolve it as soon
      as possible after the end of the voting period.
