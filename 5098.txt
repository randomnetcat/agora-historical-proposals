The first agoran week of each agoran year that falls entirely in
August is Rape the Ruleset Week. If a player submits a proposal during
that week whose sole effect is to repeal one or more rules, and that
proposal later passes, that player shall earn one VC of each of the
following colors: Red, Blue, Green. These VCs are in addition to any
other VCs that player might earn from the passage of said proposal.
