Title: Secure contract adjustments
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2.0

Amend Rule 2197 by replacing this text:

      A Contract Change can be one or more of any of the following:

      (a) a person who intends to be bound by a contract becoming a
          party to the contract;

      (b) a person ceasing to be a party to the contract;

      (c) amending a contract; and

      (d) terminating a contract


with this text:

      A Contract Change can be one or more of any of the following:

      (a) a person who intends to be bound by a contract becoming a
          party to the contract;

      (b) a person ceasing to be a party to the contract;

      (c) amending a contract;

      (d) terminating a contract;

      (e) a change to holdings of a class of assets defined by the
          contract; and

      (f) a change to any other property defined by the contract
