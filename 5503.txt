ID: 5503
Title: No elections for imposed offices
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2154 by replacing the text reading:

     Any player CAN by announcement nominate one or more active
     players to be candidate(s) for an Office, this begins a
     nomination period for that office, during which other candidates
     may be so nominated.

with:

     Any player CAN by announcement nominate one or more active
     players to be candidate(s) for an elected Office. This begins a
     nomination period for that office, during which other candidates
     may be so nominated.
