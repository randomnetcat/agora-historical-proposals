ID: 5327
Title: Allow empty contests
Author: Murphy
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 1

Amend Rule 1742 (Contracts) by replacing this text:

                                                            A contract
      automatically terminates if the number of parties to it falls
      below two.

with this text:

                                                            A contract
      automatically terminates if the number of parties to it falls
      below two (or, for contests, one).
