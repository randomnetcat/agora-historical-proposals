Title: Rubicon Rectification
Author: root
Co-authors: 
Adoption index: 2

Amend Rule 2177 (The Senate) by replacing the text:

      During emergency session, any Senator declare a filibuster on a
      proposal in its voting period, with 2 supporting Senators,
      provided no filibuster has been declared on that proposal in the
      past.  Any Senator may end a filibuster on a proposal with 4
      supporting Senators.

with:

      During emergency session, any Senator CAN declare a filibuster
      on a proposal in its voting period, with 2 supporting Senators,
      provided no filibuster has been declared on that proposal in the
      past.  Any Senator CAN end a filibuster on a proposal with 4
      supporting Senators.
